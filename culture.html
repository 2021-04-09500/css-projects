how to cook Ugali

  1. pour water in a clean alminium pan
  2. heat the water on a stove to a moderate temperature
  3. pour reasonable amount of flour in the moderately boiled water
  4. stir the mixture until well mixed
  5. keep stiring till the mixture starts to boil
  6. When appropriately cooked, add another cup, full of flour
  7. repeat the procedure above until the mixture is heavy and thoroughly stired. depending on how you want the ugali to be
  8. When well cooked, serve while hot
  9. serve with a side dish, adviced to serve with well cooked dagaa (finger licking good)
